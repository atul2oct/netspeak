## 📌 About The Project

Netspeak Infra Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a modern telecom infrastructure company specializing in IT, Telecom, and Energy solutions. This website represents the company’s services, expertise, and contact platform for clients.

The platform is designed to showcase telecom infrastructure services such as In-Building Wireless Solutions (IBWS), Rooftop Antenna Systems, and end-to-end network deployment.

---

## 🏢 About Company

Founded in **2025**, Netspeak Infra Solutions Pvt Ltd is a Bangalore-based startup with **16+ years of industry expertise** in telecom infrastructure.

### 🎯 Aim
To provide seamless connectivity and become a leading telecom infrastructure provider.

### 👁 Vision

### 🎯 Mission
To offer reliable, scalable, and efficient telecom services enabling businesses to grow.
